# Research

# PDI is centered around a collection of long-term research questions.

The implementation exists to explore these questions rather than define them.

Many of these questions may take years to answer.

Some may never have a complete answer.

That is expected.

---

# Research Philosophy

Technology changes.

Research questions remain.

PDI does not begin with implementation.

It begins with questions about people, digital life, memory, ownership, and representation.

Every major architectural decision should answer a research question.

Not the other way around.

---

# Research Questions

## RQ1 — Reality → Digital Life

How can real-world experiences become meaningful digital representations?

How can digital systems observe reality without requiring people to manually record everything?

---

## RQ2 — Human Digital Representation

What is the smallest meaningful unit of a person's digital life?

Should a digital life be represented by files, events, assets, relationships, or something else?

---

## RQ3 — Long-term Digital Memory

How should personal digital memory evolve over years rather than conversations?

How can memory remain meaningful as a person's life continuously changes?

---

## RQ4 — Memory Ownership

Who should own digital memory?

How can AI understand people without owning their memories?

How can people remain independent from individual AI products?

---

## RQ5 — Knowledge Organization

How should relationships emerge naturally from digital life?

Can meaningful knowledge structures be built without relying on manually maintained folders, tags, or notes?

---

## RQ6 — Identity

How should digital systems determine that different digital objects belong to the same person, event, or concept?

What defines identity in a long-term digital life?

---

## RQ7 — Retrieval

How should AI retrieve information from decades of personal digital history?

How can retrieval balance completeness, relevance, and efficiency?

---

## RQ8 — Trust & Evidence

How can AI explain why it believes something about a person?

What evidence should support every important conclusion?

How can trust be built instead of assumed?

---

# Current Priorities

The current stage of PDI focuses primarily on:

- Reality → Digital Life
- Digital Asset Representation
- Identity
- Knowledge Organization
- Retrieval
- Evidence

Other questions remain open and will evolve as the project grows.

---

# Open Questions

This document intentionally contains more questions than answers.

Whenever a question is answered, new questions usually appear.

PDI is expected to evolve continuously.

The goal is not to eliminate uncertainty.

The goal is to better understand it.
